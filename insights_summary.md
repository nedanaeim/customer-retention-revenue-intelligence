# Insights Summary

## Executive Findings

- Total delivered-order revenue is 15,422,461.77 across 96,478 delivered orders.
- The dataset contains 93,358 unique delivered-order customers.
- Average order value is 159.85.
- Only 3.00% of customers purchased more than once.
- 59.05% of customers are inactive or at churn risk using a 180-day inactivity threshold.

## Product Insights

The top revenue categories are:

1. health_beauty
2. watches_gifts
3. bed_bath_table
4. sports_leisure
5. computers_accessories

These categories should be the first candidates for lifecycle marketing, cross-sell bundles, and customer reactivation tests because they combine strong customer demand with meaningful revenue scale.

## Regional Insights

The strongest states by revenue are Sao Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, Minas Gerais, Rio Grande do Sul, and Parana. Sao Paulo is the clear priority market because it contributes the largest order volume and revenue base.

## Recommendations

1. Create a first-to-second purchase programme focused on customers in high-volume states.
2. Use category-specific offers for high-demand categories instead of generic discounting.
3. Segment win-back campaigns by inactivity period, value segment, and previous category purchased.
4. Add monthly tracking for repeat purchase rate, cohort retention, average order value, and churn-risk customer share.
5. Investigate review score and delivery performance by state to find operational causes of weak retention.
