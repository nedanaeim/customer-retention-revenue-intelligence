# Business Problem

Olist needs a clearer view of customer retention and revenue quality across its ecommerce marketplace. Sales volume alone does not show whether customers are becoming loyal, whether specific states or categories are healthier than others, or whether the business is relying too heavily on one-time purchases.

## Objective

Build an analytics workflow that converts raw marketplace data into decision-ready outputs for revenue, retention, customer value, product category performance, and regional performance.

## Questions Answered

- How much revenue did delivered orders generate?
- What share of customers purchased more than once?
- Which customers are most valuable or at risk of inactivity?
- Which product categories and states contribute the most revenue?
- What dashboard datasets should an analyst provide to stakeholders?

## Audience

The intended audience is a commercial, retention, or ecommerce leadership team. The project is written as a portfolio piece for data analyst roles, so the documentation explains both the technical workflow and the business meaning of the results.
